INFOSEC GIRLS IS
a community for women passionate about information security

OUR AIM
Our main objective is to get educate and train middle school students, women and everyone curious about information security.
HOW WE ENCOURAGE WOMEN
By encouraging more women to actively participate in events like security conferences and community meet-ups.
HOW YOU CAN HELP
Spread the word about us on social media. Volunteer with us to conduct workshops. Get in touch with us if you want us to conduct events at your organisation.

WHY INFOSEC GIRLS

The field of information security lacks a community for women to provide support and mentorship within the community, as well as actively encourage women in other fields interested in security.

OUR AIM

Our main objective is to get women curious about information security. We aim to do this by encouraging more women to actively participate in events like security conferences & community meet-ups.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Spreading the word about us on social media!(@infosecgirls,#infosecgirls). Volunteer with us to conduct workshops and sessions for women in technology and colleges.
